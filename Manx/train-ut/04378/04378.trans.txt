04378-001 TA MEE GOBBRAGHEY AYNS STRAID ATHOL AGH CHA MIE LHIAM MY OBBYR MONNEY ER YN OYR DY VEL EH BEGGAN DREE
04378-002 HIE MEE DYS BUNSCOILL EC RAAD MURRAY AS LURG SHE'N HIE MEE DYS NOO NINIAN
04378-003 REN MEE SHENNAGHYS GAELG AS BAARLE EC LEVELA AS LURG SHE'N HIE MEE DYS OLLOSCOILL AYNS DUN EDIN AYNS NALBIN V'EH YINDYSSAGH AYNS DUN EDIN AS TA FOAST RAM CAARJYN AYM
04378-004 CUMMAL AYNS NALBIN TA MEE CUMMAL MARISH MY VUMMIG AS MY YISHAG AYNS IMMAN WOODBOURNE AGH TA MEE GEEARREE GEDDYN THIE MARISH MY CHAARJYN AYNS KIONDROGHAD AGH ATREIH
04378-006 TA SHUYR AS BRAAR AYM TA MY VRAAR ILLIAM CUMMAL AS GOBBRAGHEY AYNS LUNNIN AS NY KEAYRTYN TA MEE CUR SHILLEY ER
04378-007 T'EH CUMMAL AYNS CHARLTON AS T'EH GOBBRAGHEY EC YN THIE-LHEIHYS AYNS SHE'N SHE FERLHEE ESHYN
04378-008 CHA MIE LESH ILLIAM LUNNIN AS T'EH GEEARREE CHEET ERASH DYS MANNIN
04378-009 TA MY HUYR JONEE CUMMAL AYNS DIVLIN SHE BENYNSEE ISH AS T'EE POOSIT RISH DOOINNEY VOISH NERIN
04378-010 S'MIE LHEE DIVLIN AS S'MIE LHIAM GOLL DYS NERIN NISH AS REESHT
04378-011 S'MIE LHIAM GEAISHTAGH RISH KIAULL AS S'MIE LHIAM JEEAGHYN ER FILMYN TA MEE GOLL MAGH ER Y JOUGH MARISH MY CHAARJYN DAGH OIE JEHEINEY TA SHIN GOLL MYGEAYRT DOOLISH AGH SHARE LHIAM GOLL DYS THIE-YN-LHIONNEY
04378-012 GOLLRISH THE ROVERS AS YN THIE BANE AYNS PURT NY H'INSHEY S'MIE LHIAM GEAISHTAGH RISH KIAULL MANNINAGH AS NY KEAYRTYN TA MEE CLOIE YN GUITAR AGH CHA NEL MEE FEER VIE
