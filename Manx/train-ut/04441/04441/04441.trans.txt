04441-000 MISH PAAIE KERRUISH AS TA MEE CUMMAL AYNS Y JIASS S'MIE LHIAM YN JIASS AGH SHARE LHIAM YN TWOAIE
04441-001 VA MEE AYNS Y TWOAIE JIU REN MEE GOLL DYS Y TWOAIE SON CUR SHILLEY ER JONEE SMITH
04441-002 TA JONEE CUMMAL AYNS SULBY AS S'MIE LHEE CUMMAL AYNS SULBY
04441-003 ERLHIAM DY VEL SULBY MIE DY LIOOAR AGH SHARE LHIAM RHUMSAA
04441-004 SHE GAELGEYR JONEE AS S'MIE LHIAM LOAYRT RISH JONEE AYNS GAELG ANSHERBEE LURG CUR SHILLEY ER JONEE REN MEE GOLL DYS SHAKTI MANN AS REN MEE KIONNAGHEY RAM STOO
04441-005 LURG SHAPPAL AYNS SHE'N REN MEE GOLL DYS DOOLISH SON CUR SHILLEY ER CARREY ELLEY BILLY CAIN
04441-006 TA CUMMAL AYNS POYLL ROISH
04441-007 S'MIE LHIAM BILLY AS TA RAM GAELG ECHEY AGH CHA MIE LHIAM DOOLISH
04441-008 VA MEE STHIE RIYR AS REN MEE GEAISHTAGH RISH RADIO KIARE
04441-009 S'MIE LHIAM GEAISHTAGH RISH YN RADIO AS S'MIE LHIAM YN NAIGHT ER RADIO KIARE
04441-010 REN MEE COAGYREY AS LURG SHE'N REN MEE LOAYRT ER Y CHELLVANE RISH CARREY ELLEY BARBARA TA CUMMAL AYNS SOSTYN
04441-011 T'EE CUMMAL AYNS BIRMINGHAM NISH AGH SHE MANNINAGH ISH DY JARROO SHE SPAAINAGH VOISH PURT LE MOIRREY ISH SHE YNSEYDER BARBARA AS S'MIE LHEE CUMMAL AS G'OBBRAGH AYNS
04441-012 BIRMINGHAM AGH T'EE GEEARREE CHEET ERASH DYS MANNIN
04441-013 ANSHERBEE BEEYM EC OBBYR MAIRAGH AYNS DOOLISH AGH BEEYM ERASH AYNS PURT LE MOIRREY REESHT MYSH QUEIG
04441-014 ERLHIAM DY BEEYM EC YN ALBERT OIE NY VAIRAGH SON JOUGH
04441-015 S'MIE LHIAM GIU USHTEY VEA NISH AS REESHT AGH SHARE LHIAM GIU BEGGAN EC YN ALBERT
