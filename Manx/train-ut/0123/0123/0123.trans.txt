0123-003 BENTYN DA KEESH SON KIED DY VARROO USHAGYN

0123-005 SAYMMANNAGH RE DY VODDAGH FER NY JEES NY VUD OC TA MIANDAGH ER BEE BLAYSTAL AS FEIEYS GOAILL ASS LAUE DY VODDAGH AD JANNOO GAMMAN JIN
0123-006 SHICKYR DY LIOOAR SY TRAA T'ER N'GHOLL SHAGHEY
0123-007 VA LHEID Y ROOSTEYRYS SURRT TRA HAGHYR DA VE AIGNEY BAANRIT R'EE NY CHIARN ENNAGH SON FREAYLL OOILLEY NY REDDYN MIE DA HENE

0123-009 CIRCLE NAGH VEL EH LOWAL JEH CHAMOO VEES EH SPOOILLIT NOI E YIOIN

0123-016 VEL SHOH RESOON MY HESHEE GHEINEY N'EE SHOH CAIRYS
0123-017 JIR SHIU DY VEL LHEID Y REAMYS LEIGHOIL NY DY LHISAGH EH VE JEANT LEIGHOIL CHOUD AS TA BREE MEEYL FAAGIT AYNS MANNINEE
0123-018 MEEYL DOOYRT MEE SHE BEISHTEIG SINSHLEY NA MEEYL YINNAGH SURRANSE LHEID YN OLTOOAN
0123-019 SON OLTOOAN HROME EH DY GHRA YN CHOOID SLOO JEH DY VE SOIT ORRIN MYR SHE'N AYNS NY'N EER MAGHERYN AS BLHOYS LHIEN KIONNAGHEY GREIEBAAISH
0123-020 SON NY HYMMYDYN LOWAL AIN HENE FEGOOISH HOSHIAGHT SHIRREY KIED ERQUOI
0123-021 AS MY HAGHYRYS DA GUIYFEIE NY THUNNAG NY PATRAG NY EER LHON GETLAGH SHAGHEY
0123-022 SHEGIN FAAGAIL EH DA'N CHIED JOARREE DA'N CHIED VAARLIAGH HED HARRISH NY'N GAGLIAGH
0123-023 VEAGH SHOH NAAREYDAGH LHIGGEY LESH AYNS CHEER ERBEE FODDEY SMOO AYNS CHEER NY LEIGHYN ECK TA RESOONAGH AS FOLLAN AS
0123-024 MIETA CUR SLANE REAMYS DA DY CHOOILLEY UNNANE JANNOO CRE SAILLISH RISH E CHOOID HENE
0123-025 AGH CHA VEL Y LEIGH NOA SHOH ENNEE FOAST ER VE LOWIT JEH LIORISH Y R'EE
0123-026 CHAMOO CREDJYM DY BEE EH
0123-027 AS T'EH YINDYS VOOAR LHIAM'S DY ROW EH RIEAU ER NY CHUMMEY ER CHEUSTHIE JEH'N ELLAN
0123-028 N'EE YN CHIAREASFEED ODDAGH CUR NY'N LAUE DYS LHEID Y LEIGH
0123-029 N'EE SLANE OOASHLEY YN ELLAN GHOGHE ASS LAUE LHEID Y RED
0123-030 AGH MY GHEINEY CHEEREY NY SURRJEE LHEID DY VE JEANT RIU
0123-032 TA PAART ER N'YANNOO SHOH AS TAR AD CHOUD SHE'N CREENEY
